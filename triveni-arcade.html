Triveni Arcade

Commercial
2006

Enter a shopping paradise at Triveni Arcade, the first shopping arcade on Vallabh Vidhyanagar Road, which features a stunning complex of shops, including Santushti, SGB, Belgian Waffles, Motilal Oswal, Xiaomi, and many more. Our arcade features contemporary designs, industrial-standard top-of-the-line materials, and exceptional craftsmanship, all coming together for an exceptional shopping experience.
