Triveni Landmark

Residential
2022

Triveni Landmark, where we've combined modern, tasteful construction with first-rate eco-friendly planning to create a living experience like no other. From functionality to following construction guidelines, creating a space that is not only beautiful, but also keeping environment at heart to make up a perfect living. Our project features, well-built housing units of three unique types, nestled among a stunning 21-acre landscape - the perfect place to call home with the following amenities available:

  • Street Lights For All Common Amenities
  • Gorgeous Fountains
  • 11 Theme-based Gardens
  • Elevated Service Reservoir
  • Special Car Park For Visitors
  • Special Eco-friendly Vehicles To Commute Visitors
  • Jogging Track & Children’s Play Area
