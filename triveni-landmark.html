Triveni Landmark

DATE: May 2018

Triveni Landmark is a symbol of modern and tasteful construction combined with first-rate eco-friendly planning that includes everything from colour selection to building materials. All aspects of the designs and requirements have been given careful consideration.

The project consists of individual well-built housing units of three types spread across a beautiful 21-acre landscape.

Triveni Landmark stands tall, beckoning visitors to its tranquil surroundings. It includes the most beautiful elevations while remaining easily maintainable. As visitors enter Triveni Landmark, they are greeted by magnificent fountains. There is ample parking for individual units, as well as consideration for your valued guests.


Address:
201,Triveni Shopping Arcade, Anand Vidyanagar Road, Anand - 388001, Gujarat, India.
